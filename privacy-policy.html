Unsere Website wird bei Hostinger gehostet. Zur schnelleren Auslieferung der Inhalte wird ein Content Delivery Network (CDN) von Cloudflare genutzt, das Daten global verteilt. Es kann dabei zu Übermittlungen in Länder außerhalb der EU kommen. Diese Übermittlungen erfolgen auf Grundlage von Standardvertragsklauseln (Art. 46 DSGVO).

Eingesetzte Dienste und Tools auf der Website
  • Google Analytics (Tracking & Nutzungsanalyse)
  • Facebook Pixel (Marketing & Conversion-Messung)
  • Unsplash Usage Statistics (Bilder-Statistik)
  • Astro Framework Usage Statistics (technische Nutzungsanalyse)
  • OpenResty Usage Statistics (Serverstatistiken)

Rechtsgrundlage:

  • Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. a DSGVO (Einwilligung) für Tracking- und Marketingtools
  • Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. f DSGVO (berechtigtes Interesse) für technische Statistik

4. Datenerfassung in unserer App

Erfasste Daten
  • GPS-Standortdaten: zur Durchführung des Spiels und Erstellung einer Spielaufzeichnung
  • Video-Uploads: hochgeladene Inhalte werden 24 Stunden gespeichert
  • Sentry (Fehlertracking): anonyme Sitzungsdaten zur Fehleranalyse

Serverstandorte

Rechtsgrundlage:

  • Art. 6 Abs. 1 lit. a DSGVO (Einwilligung bei optionalen Funktionen)

Erfolgt nur, wenn dies für die Vertragserfüllung oder aufgrund Ihrer Einwilligung notwendig ist.

Sämtliche Übermittlungen erfolgen gemäß den Vorgaben der Art. 44 ff. DSGVO und auf Basis von Standardvertragsklauseln.

Privacy Policy for Website and App

Chasing Reality UG (haftungsbeschränkt)

Email: hej@thechase.site
Data Protection Officer: Leonhard Straub

2. General Information

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy informs you about what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

This policy applies to both our Website and our Mobile App "Chasing Reality".

3. Data Collection on Our Website

Hosting & CDN

Our website is hosted by Hostinger. For faster content delivery, we use a Content Delivery Network (CDN) provided by Cloudflare, which distributes data globally. This may involve transfers to countries outside the EU, which are based on Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46 GDPR).

Services and Tools Used on the Website
  • Google Analytics (tracking & usage analysis)
  • Facebook Pixel (marketing & conversion tracking)
  • Unsplash Usage Statistics (image statistics)
  • Astro Framework Usage Statistics (technical usage analysis)
  • OpenResty Usage Statistics (server statistics)

Legal basis:

  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent) for tracking and marketing tools
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest) for technical statistics

4. Data Collection in Our App

Data Collected
  • GPS location data: to run the game and create a game replay
  • Video uploads: uploaded content stored for 48 hours
  • Sentry (error tracking): anonymous session data for bug analysis
  • Google Maps: for map display
  • Firebase: app hosting and analytics
Server Locations
  • Functional app servers: Frankfurt, Germany
  • Storage of videos & location data: Belgium
  • No sharing with third parties except the listed providers

Legal basis:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract)
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent for optional features)

5. Newsletter

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address and, if provided, your name.

You can unsubscribe at any time.

6. Contact Form

Data collected through our contact form (name, email, message) will only be used to process your request.

7. Storage Periods

  • GPS data & videos: 24 hours
  • Contact data: until the request is resolved
  • Newsletter data: until withdrawal
  • Analytics data: according to tool-specific retention periods

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR)

9. Data Transfers to Third Countries

Only if necessary for contract fulfilment or based on your consent.

All transfers are carried out in compliance with Art. 44 et seq. GDPR and based on Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Security

We use technical and organisational measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, and unauthorised access.

11. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy to reflect legal or technical changes.

12. Account Deletion

To delete your user data, send an email from the email address associated with your account. In your message, simply state that you wish to have your user data deleted. We will process your request promptly and remove all your personal data.
